Our Garden

Samanea saman

Urakkamthoongi Maram

The Rain Tree is a giant shade tree known for its umbrella-shaped canopy and "sleepy" leaves that fold during rain or at night.

Mangifera indica

Mavu

The Mango Tree is an evergreen known as the King of Fruits. It provides dense shade and can live for over 300 years.

Guva

Guva

A hardy tropical tree famous for its nutritious fruit and medicinal leaves, highly valued for health benefits.

Catharanthus roseus

Nithyakalyani

The Madagascar Periwinkle is a beautiful flowering plant that blooms year-round and has significant medicinal properties.

Manilkara zapota

Chikoo

Produces sweet, brown fruits with a grainy texture. This evergreen is a staple in tropical orchards.

Coffea arabica

Coffee

A glossy-leaved evergreen that thrives under the shade, producing the beans for our favorite morning brew.

Cocos nucifera

Coconut Tree

A tall palm widely cultivated in Kerala. It bears coconuts throughout the year and is the tree of life.

Syzygium cumini

Jamun

Jamun is a large evergreen tree native to India. It bears deep purple fruits with medicinal value.

Cycas revoluta

Sago Palm

A slow-growing ornamental plant with a thick trunk and stiff, feather-like leaves arranged in a crown.

Terminalia catappa

Betham

A large tropical tree with almond-like seeds. Commonly planted for its expansive shade.

Citrus limon

Lemon

A small evergreen tree grown for its citrus fruit and medicinal properties.

Lawsonia inermis

Henna

Henna is a small shrub or tree with thin branches and small opposite leaves. Its leaves are used to make natural dye for body art and hair coloring. The plant is also known for its medicinal and cooling properties.

Muntingia calabura

Indian Cherry

Indian Cherry is a small to medium-sized fast-growing tree with serrated, alternate leaves. It produces small red edible fruits that are sweet and attractive to birds. The tree is commonly planted in schools and open areas for shade and quick growth.

Ficus religiosa

Peepal Tree

Peepal is a large deciduous tree with heart-shaped leaves and a long tapering tip. It is considered sacred in India and commonly found near schools, temples, and roadsides. The tree is known for its longevity, shade, and environmental importance.

Ficus microcarpa

Indian Laurel

Indian Laurel is a large evergreen fig tree with small, smooth, glossy leaves. It forms a dense canopy and is widely planted in campuses and public places for shade. Unlike the Banyan, it usually lacks prominent aerial roots.

Delonix regia

Gulmohar

Gulmohar is a fast-growing ornamental tree with feathery bipinnate leaves. It is famous for its bright red flowers during summer. The tree provides light shade and is widely planted along roads and campuses.

Polyalthia longifolia

Ashoka Tree

Ashoka tree is a tall, evergreen ornamental tree with long, narrow, glossy leaves. It is commonly planted along roadsides and in campuses for its straight growth and dense foliage

Calophyllum inophyllum

Alexandrian laurel

Calophyllum inophyllum is an evergreen tree with thick, glossy, opposite leaves showing distinct parallel veins. It is commonly found in coastal and tropical regions of India and South Asia. The tree is valued for its medicinal oil, shade, and ornamental beauty.

Banana plant

Musa paradisiaca

The banana plant is a large perennial herb with broad, elongated leaves and a false trunk made of tightly packed leaf bases. It grows rapidly in tropical climates and produces banana bunches after flowering.

Aegle marmelos

Koovalam

Koovalam is a sacred deciduous tree with trifoliate aromatic leaves. It is associated with Lord Shiva and widely grown in India. Its leaves and fruits are used in Ayurveda for digestive disorders.

Tectona grandis

Teak

A single, straight, cylindrical trunk; the root system is typically superficial lateral roots and a taproot, but no aerial roots
